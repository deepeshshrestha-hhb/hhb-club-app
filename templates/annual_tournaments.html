HHB Annual Tournaments

All of the club's annual competitions, by type.

Annual Championships

Pool A & Pool B knockouts. First edition in 2026.

16–28 players divided by ability into Pool A and Pool B. Same-pool pairing: Pool A players pair within Pool A, Pool B players pair within Pool B. Winner and Runner-ups from both pools compete for 8 trophies.

Annual Doubles Classic

Running since 2018 (no edition in 2020).

20–28 players divided by ability into Pool A and Pool B. Cross-pool pairing: Pool A players pair with Pool B players to form balanced teams. Group stage and knockout competition with 6 trophies for winners, runners-up, and 3rd place.

View All Years
Annual Players League

EPL-style season league. 2022–2024, returning in 2026.

10-week season (Sep–Nov) with all players participating by default. Rankings based on reported match scores. Top 3 positions win trophies.
